Section 01 — The Product

Meet the cocktail in a packet

Chill Sip is a range of premium ready-to-mix cocktail sachets. Real fruit flavours, real spirits, real fun — engineered so anyone can serve a perfect drink in seconds.

Every Chill Sip sachet contains a single-serve cocktail concentrate crafted by our mixologists. Tear, pour over ice, and top with your choice of mixer. No shakers, no syrup bottles, no training needed. Each packet is portioned perfectly so every guest gets the same great drink — from the first round to the last.

Available in a rotating seasonal lineup — from tropical Pineapple Fizz and Passion Fruit Margarita to classic Strawberry Daiquiri — Chill Sip brings colour, taste, and vibe to any occasion. Our summer collection was built for high-energy settings where speed and quality matter equally.
