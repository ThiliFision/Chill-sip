Cocktail Recipes

Each recipe uses one Rip & Sip syrup. Rip the packet, follow the steps, own the night.

Pineapple & Pisco
⏱ 3 min
Pisco Sour Remix
The classic Pisco Sour elevated with our tropical pineapple concentrate. Sharp, creamy and dangerously smooth.
Ingredients
  • Pineapple & Pisco Syrup 1 packet (50ml)
  • Pisco 60ml
  • Fresh lime juice 25ml
  • Egg white 1
  • Ice cubes handful
  • Angostura bitters 2 dashes
Method
  1. Dry shake egg white, syrup and lime juice for 20 seconds.
  2. Add ice and pisco, shake hard for 15 seconds.
  3. Double-strain into a coupe glass.
  4. Drop 2 dashes of bitters on the foam and swirl.
Uses: Pineapple & Pisco

Passion Fruit
⏱ 1 min
Tropical Fizz
Zero effort, maximum tropical. Rip the packet into a glass of sparkling water and you're done. Festival perfect.
Ingredients
  • Passion Fruit Syrup 1 packet (30ml)
  • Sparkling water 200ml
  • Fresh mint 3–4 leaves
  • Ice lots
  • Lime slice to garnish
Method
  1. Fill a highball glass to the top with ice.
  2. Rip the packet and pour the syrup over the ice.
  3. Top with sparkling water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with mint and a lime slice. Done.
Uses: Passion Fruit Fusion

Passion Fruit
⏱ 4 min
Exotic Daiquiri
Passion fruit daiquiri with a velvet finish. White rum, citrus and our syrup — shaken and served straight up.
Ingredients
  • Passion Fruit Syrup 1 packet (50ml)
  • White rum 60ml
  • Fresh lime juice 30ml
  • Triple sec 15ml
  • Ice for shaking
  • Passion fruit half to garnish
Method
  1. Combine syrup, rum, lime and triple sec in a shaker.
  2. Fill with ice and shake vigorously for 15 seconds.
  3. Double-strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. Float a passion fruit half on top to serve.
Uses: Passion Fruit Fusion

Lemon Crash
⏱ 1 min
Lemon Drop Shot
One rip, one shot. Lemon Crash syrup meets ice-cold vodka for the most refreshing shot you've ever taken.
Ingredients
  • Lemon Crash Syrup 1 packet (30ml)
  • Vodka 30ml
  • Sugar rim optional
  • Lemon zest to garnish
Method
  1. Chill the shot glass in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  2. Rim the glass with sugar if desired.
  3. Mix syrup and vodka directly in the glass.
  4. Finish with a twist of lemon zest and shoot.
Uses: Lemon Crash

Strawberry & Mint
⏱ 3 min
Strawberry Mint Smash
Muddled mint, crushed ice and our Strawberry Lemon syrup over gin. Looks like summer, tastes even better.
Ingredients
  • Strawberry Lemon & Mint Syrup 1 packet (50ml)
  • London Dry Gin 50ml
  • Fresh mint 8 leaves
  • Lemon juice 15ml
  • Crushed ice to fill
  • Soda water splash
Method
  1. Muddle mint leaves gently in a rocks glass.
  2. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  3. Add gin, syrup and lemon juice over the ice.
  4. Top with a splash of soda and stir. Garnish with mint sprig.
Uses: Strawberry Lemon & Mint

Lemon Crash
⏱ 2 min
Citrus Paloma
Tequila, grapefruit and Lemon Crash — the Paloma gets a serious upgrade. Salt rim, ice cold, non-negotiable.
Ingredients
  • Lemon Crash Syrup 1 packet (30ml)
  • Blanco tequila 50ml
  • Grapefruit soda 150ml
  • Salt for rim
  • Ice large cubes
  • Grapefruit slice garnish
Method
  1. Salt the rim of a highball glass.
  2. Add large ice cubes to the glass.
  3. Pour tequila and syrup over the ice and stir briefly.
  4. Top with grapefruit soda and garnish. Serve immediately.
Uses: Lemon Crash

All Flavours
⏱ 10 min · 20 servings
Festival Punch
The crowd pleaser. Mix a full batch of all four syrups together and let the festival do the rest. Serves 20.
Ingredients (Batch)
  • Pineapple & Pisco Syrup 500ml bottle
  • Passion Fruit Syrup 500ml bottle
  • White rum 700ml
  • Prosecco 2 bottles
  • Pineapple juice 1L
  • Ice block 1 large
Method
  1. Combine syrups, rum and pineapple juice in a large punch bowl.
  2. Stir well and chill for 30 minutes before serving.
  3. Add the ice block just before guests arrive.
  4. Top with prosecco when serving and ladle into cups.
Uses: All Syrups

All Flavours
⏱ 2 min · 4 shots
Neon Shot Train
Four packets. Four shot glasses. Four flavours. Line them up, rip them, shoot them. Repeat as required.
Ingredients (Per Round)
  • Pineapple & Pisco Syrup 1 packet
  • Passion Fruit Syrup 1 packet
  • Lemon Crash Syrup 1 packet
  • Strawberry & Mint Syrup 1 packet
  • Vodka 30ml per shot
Method
  1. Line up four chilled shot glasses.
  2. Pour 30ml vodka into each glass.
  3. Rip a different syrup packet into each glass.
  4. Pass them out, count down from 3, and go.
Uses: All 4 Syrups

⏱ 1 min · 1 serving
Pineapple Spritz
The fastest festival drink alive. One packet into a cup of ice and prosecco. That's it. That's the recipe.
Ingredients
  • Pineapple & Pisco Syrup 1 packet (30ml)
  • Prosecco or Cava 150ml
  • Ice to fill
  • Mint sprig optional
Method
  1. Fill a plastic cup or wine glass with ice.
  2. Rip the packet and pour in the syrup.
  3. Top with chilled prosecco.
  4. Give it one stir and hand it to someone who deserves it.
Uses: Pineapple & Pisco
